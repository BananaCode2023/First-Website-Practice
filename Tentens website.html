About Us

Ten-Ten's Barbeque Corner, the Mataasnakahoy's Grill Expert, is known to customers as the brand that delightfully serves Ihaw-Sarap food and Unli-Saya experience for the past 2 decades.


From a local provincce, Ten-Ten's was established on December 12, 2003 in Mataasnakahoy, Batangas by "Boss Idol". Apart from the delicious, craveable taste of its barbeques that has won the hearts of Filipinos, Ten-Ten's was also the first to offer barbeque in tricycle in Mataasnakahoy Batangas. Over the years, the brand has garnered numerous awards as a fast-growing local food company, an outstanding quick service restaurant (QSR), and an excellent Filipino restaurant.


"Boss Idol" is a very hardworking and inspiring cook, owner, father, and business owner. He started selling barbeques with his own food cart around streets, schools, and different places. The heat, rain, and disasters doesn't budge him to quit and stop. Achieving his dreams for his family and his business is his top priority and never fails to impress which can be tasted in his foods.



As of today, Ten-Ten's Barbeque corner is thriving and is still selling the best barbeque in Mataasnakahoy. Even the owner has a disability, this won't hinder him or the business to be successful.
